Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with HopeBridge?

Fill out our volunteer form on the Volunteer page. Our team will contact you within 48 hours to discuss available programs in your city.

Are donations tax deductible?

Yes! HopeBridge Foundation is registered under 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a certificate for 50% tax exemption on your donation amount.

How do I know my donation is being used properly?

We publish annual impact reports and quarterly fund utilization statements available on request. We maintain 100% financial transparency across all operations.

Can organizations partner with HopeBridge for CSR?

Absolutely! We have dedicated CSR partnership programs. Contact us at info@hopebridgefoundation.org with your requirements.
